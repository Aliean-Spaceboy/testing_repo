This application is designed to take you from a complete beginner (A1) to professional fluency (B2). To get the fastest results, follow this daily routine.

⏱️ The Optimal Daily Routine (20-30 Minutes)

  1. Check the Dashboard (2 mins): Read the Word and Sentence of the Day. Click the "Listen" button and repeat the pronunciation out loud.
  2. Review Vocabulary & SRS (5 mins): Open More ? SRS Review. Review flashcards due today. Add 5 to 10 new words you encountered at work to the Vocabulary tab.
  3. Write Your Diary (10 mins): Open the Diary tab. Do the Sentence Builder prompt first. Answer the 3 standard prompts. When saving, strictly follow the Grammar Checklist.
  4. Take a Quick Quiz (5 mins): Alternate daily: MCQ (Mon/Wed), Listening (Tue/Thu), Grammar & Verbs (Fri).
💡 Weekly Habit: Every Sunday, open the Weekly Reflection tab to review your progress, and export a Data Backup from your Dashboard to keep your words safe!

📈 How to Adapt Your Routine for Each Level

🟢 A1 Level (Beginner)

Goal: Build basic vocabulary and get used to sounds.

  • Vocab: Add everyday words (Haus, essen). Pay attention to Gender Colors (Blue = der, Red = die, Green = das).
  • Diary: Just write 1 simple sentence per prompt (e.g., Ich lerne Deutsch).
  • Tools: Use the Sentence Builder manually and test the Pronunciation Checker.

🟡 A2 Level (Elementary)

Goal: Connect sentences and understand spoken German.

  • Vocab: Group words into categories (Verbs, Daily Life).
  • Diary: Write 2 sentences per prompt. Combine them using und, aber, weil.
  • Tools: Focus heavily on the Dictation Quiz to train your ear.

🟠 B1 Level (Intermediate)

Goal: Professional vocabulary and holding conversations.
